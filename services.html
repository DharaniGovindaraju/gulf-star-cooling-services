Services

We are experts in industrial HVAC installations and in district cooling plants.

We offer complete HVAC project implementation from the design and engineering stage, through to the procurement, installation, commissioning and maintenance of various HVAC systems.

Complete HVAC Project Implementation



Our services follow our strict quality assurance and quality control procedures. We are ISO 9001:2008 certified with our management system for design, installation, commissioning and maintenance of industrial air conditioning systems.

DX Cooling Systems

Design, commissioning and maintenance of DX units (package units, central ducted split units, decorative split units, VRV units) complete with its ducting networks and electrical works

Chilled Water Systems

Design, commissioning and maintenance of chilled water networks (air cooled or water cooled), including piping, flushing and passivation of these networks

Cold Storage Room

Design, manufacturing, installation and commissioning work in cold rooms and freezers for industrial and residential projects

Extract & Ventilation Systems

Design, commissioning and maintenance of fresh air systems, central extract systems and ventilation systems
